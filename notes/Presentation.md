# Presentation Message

The reader should come away with this picture:

1. KNIME is widely used in scientific work.
2. Scientists who use KNIME rarely publish the actual workflow files, which
   weakens reproducibility.
3. KNIME continues to evolve, and the number of legacy or deprecated nodes has
   grown. Without careful version reporting and workflow preservation,
   reproducing older KNIME-based studies will become harder.
